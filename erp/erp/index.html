About ZENED

ZENED is designed for small and mid-sized colleges to digitize admissions, attendance, fees, and academic records. It supports local languages, offline sync, and mobile access for low-resource environments.

  • ✔️ Student onboarding & fee tracking
  • ✔️ Attendance & report generation
  • ✔️ Local language & offline support
  • ✔️ Mobile-friendly, modern UI
